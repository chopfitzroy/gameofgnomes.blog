== Tempo & Cadence

Talk about the use of _zoom_ in play, in combat a turn is six seconds, in dialogue it might be one minute, around town four hours and during travel several days.

Use this to move the narrative at the appropriate pace.

Zoom will be different from game to game, in a space game very little time might be spent discussing what happens at _home base_ versus travelling between the stars. Whereas a political intrigue style game that spends a lot of time focusing on urban settings probably isn't too interested in observing the time spent travelling between capitals in any great detail.
