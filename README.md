# Game of Gnomes

Hey I'm Otis and this is my little corner of the internet where I talk about TTRPG's and how I play them.

### How it works

I have worked in the web industry my entire adult life and honestly I'm burnt out, the tools we have available to us as engineers are incredible and endless.

This site is the antithesis of that endless flow of new and exciting tooling. It is archaic by design.

The site is built with [soupault](https://soupault.net/) and is deliberately small.

All [open graph](https://ogp.me/) images are generate with [ImageMagick](https://imagemagick.org/).

All content is written in [AsciiDoc](https://asciidoc.org/) and compiled with [Pandoc](https://pandoc.org/).

This site is part of the [smolweb](https://smolweb.org/) and deliberately shys away from fancy tooling.

Analytics are recorded with [GoatCounter](https://www.goatcounter.com/) and will eventually be made publicly available.

### Does it use AI

You may have noticed the `AGENTS.md` yes I do use LLM's to assist with some of the Lua code as it is a language I am still getting familiar with. I do not however use LLM's for any creative writing as I wholeheartedly believe it erases ones voice.

If you want to know more about what I think of LLM's or the tech industry in general I recommend my [personal site](https://chopfitzroy.online/).
